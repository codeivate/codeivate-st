Thanks for installing Codeivate! 

What is Codeivate?
Aiming to provide fun personal analytics for developers. Get insight into your work habits, and compete against others just by coding in Sublime.


Please see www.codeivate.com to sign up for an account and for more details.

After you create an account visit http://www.codeivate.com/token 
and add your authentication details to  "Preferences -> Package Settings -> Codeivate -> settings - user"

Feedback, bugs, ideas? please contact http://www.twitter.com/codeivate
